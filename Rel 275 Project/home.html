Website Summary

The Book of Mormon is a record of a family that left Jerusalem and lived in the Americas. This record contains this family’s, and their descendants’, dealings with God and Jesus Christ. The Book of Mormon was written under divine direction and contains many doctrines that have been lost over centuries of unbelief and corruption of God’s scriptures. The Book of Mormon was written for all to be convinced through their faith and prayers that Jesus is Christ, that the book is true, and that He remembers His covenants.


The Book of Mormon contains many lost plain and precious truths in addition to more of God’s teachings or instructions. Although one can memorize or know all of these truths and teachings, knowledge alone does little to help them. One must practice it, live it, and gain the blessings that come with living God’s teachings.
